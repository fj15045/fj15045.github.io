KovaaK's FPS Aim Trainer

What is KovaaK's?

The goal of KovaaK's FPS Aim Trainer is to enable players to create their own training with realistic dodging targets.
Every mechanic within the game is driven by user-defined profiles - Weapons, characters, abilities, bots, how bots dodge, how bots aim, challenges, and more are all configurable by hundreds of variables.
All of these profiles can be saved and distributed in a single scenario file.
Hone your muscle memory in the trainer, apply it in game.

Where to buy?

Kovaak's FPS Aim Trainer can be purchased from the steam store.
It can be purchased for a price of NZ$12.39

Supported games?

Kovaak's FPS Aim Trainer helps with various different games.
Kovaak's contains cross-hairs sensitivity sliders, Weapon bloom and recoil, Target movement and Weapon projectiles imported and recreated from different games.
These games include:
-Fortnite
-Counter Strike Global Offensive
-Quake Champions
-Overwatch
-Apex Legends
There are many crosshairs and levels that are designed for overall aim and muscle memory aswell.
